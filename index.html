We're Here to Answer All Your Questions

What are your main products?
We specialize in Self Adhesive Vinyl, PVC Flex Banner, Cutting Plotter Film, Acrylic Sheet, PVC Foam Board, and Display Equipment. We also offer custom specifications according to your requirements.
Do you offer custom sizes and specifications?
Yes, we offer custom sizes, thickness, and specifications. Please provide your detailed requirements, and our team will provide a solution tailored to your needs.
What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
Our MOQ varies by product type. Generally, it's 100 pieces for standard products. For custom orders, please contact us directly for specific requirements.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept T/T (Wire Transfer), PayPal, and Western Union. For new customers, we may require a 30% deposit with the balance paid before shipment.
How long does it take to process my order?
Production time varies by product. Standard products ship within 3-7 days. Custom orders may take 10-15 days. We'll provide an estimated delivery date upon order confirmation.
Can I request samples before placing a bulk order?
Yes, we offer free samples for standard products. Customers only need to cover the shipping cost. For custom samples, please contact us for pricing.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. We work with major shipping carriers like DHL, FedEx, UPS, and sea freight for larger orders.
What are the shipping costs?
Shipping costs depend on destination, weight, and shipping method. We provide competitive rates and will quote you the exact shipping cost when you place your order.
How long does delivery take?
Express shipping (DHL/FedEx/UPS) takes 5-7 business days. Sea freight takes 25-35 days depending on the destination country and port.
Are your products certified?
Yes, we are ISO 9001:2015 certified. Our products meet international quality standards and are exported to Europe, Americas, Southeast Asia, and Middle East markets.
Do you offer quality guarantees?
Absolutely! We offer a 100% quality guarantee. If you receive defective products, we will replace them free of charge or provide a full refund.
Can you provide test reports or samples?
Yes, we can provide test reports, certificates, and samples upon request. Our quality control team conducts thorough inspections before shipment.
