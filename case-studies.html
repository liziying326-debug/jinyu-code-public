Real-world advertising material projects delivered by Foshan Jin Yu – from single shop signs to large-scale commercial deployments.

Retail / Southeast Asia

Supermarket Sign Project

📍 Location: Southeast Asia
🏗️ Scale: 3,000 sqm supermarket, 12 branches
📦 Volume: 8,000+ sign units

A regional supermarket chain in Southeast Asia required complete interior and exterior signage solutions for 12 new branch openings across three countries. Requirements included aisle signs, price boards, promotional displays, entrance signs, and ceiling hangings.

Materials Supplied

  • ▸ PVC Foam Board 5mm / 8mm – lightweight aisle and promotional sign panels
  • ▸ Self Adhesive Vinyl 100g (Glossy) – printed graphic overlays for all sign panels
  • ▸ Aluminum Composite Panel 4mm – exterior entrance signs and façade branding
  • ▸ PP Hollow Sheet 4mm – temporary promotional signs, price boards

Project Outcomes

On-time delivery across 4 shipments within 6-week timeline
All 12 branches opened with complete sign systems
Materials passed local safety and chemical compliance testing
Client re-ordered for additional branch rollouts in 2024
Commercial / Middle East

Mall Light Box Project

📍 Location: Middle East
🏗️ Scale: 180,000 sqm shopping mall
📦 Volume: 2,400+ light box panels

A large-scale shopping mall development in the Middle East required high-quality light box panels for internal tenant signage, directional wayfinding, and promotional display boards throughout all levels of the complex.

Materials Supplied

  • ▸ Opal Acrylic Sheet 4mm / 6mm – light diffusion panels for LED backlit signs
  • ▸ Back-lit PVC Flex Banner (440g) – graphic media for illuminated advertising panels
  • ▸ LED Modules 12V – IP65 waterproof LED modules for all light box units
  • ▸ Power Supply Units – 200W switching power supplies, CE approved

Project Outcomes

Consistent even light diffusion across all 2,400+ panels
All materials passed CE / RoHS compliance documentation
Project completed in 4 phased deliveries over 8 months
Zero warranty claims in first 12 months of operation
Outdoor / Europe

Outdoor Billboard Project

📍 Location: Eastern Europe
🏗️ Scale: 350+ billboard structures
📦 Volume: 180,000 sqm flex banner

An outdoor advertising company in Eastern Europe required consistent large-format flex banner supply for their billboard network renovation program. Key requirements: high UV resistance, consistent printing performance, and compliance with EU environmental standards.

Materials Supplied

  • ▸ Front-lit Flex Banner 510g / 3.2m wide – primary billboard media
  • ▸ Aluminum Composite Panel 4mm PVDF – permanent frame panels
  • ▸ Eyelets & Reinforcement Tape – banner installation accessories

Project Outcomes

Full REACH / RoHS compliance documentation provided
Consistent color batch quality across 180,000 sqm supply
On-time sea freight delivery to 3 European ports
Ongoing annual supply contract established
Retail / Americas

Shop Sign Project

📍 Location: Latin America
🏗️ Scale: 200+ retail outlets
📦 Volume: Mixed material kit supply

A sign-making distributor in Latin America needed a reliable Chinese supplier for standardized shop sign kits to service their retail client network. Requirements included consistent quality, competitive pricing, and flexible minimum order quantities.

Materials Supplied

  • ▸ PVC Foam Board 5mm (White) – core sign panel substrate
  • ▸ Self Adhesive Vinyl 120g (Glossy + Matte) – graphic application film
  • ▸ Cold Lamination Film (Glossy / Matte) – print protection
  • ▸ Aluminum Trim Strips + Standoff Bolts – professional finishing hardware

Project Outcomes

Standardized kit format simplified distributor inventory management
Regular monthly shipments established for 200+ outlets
Custom carton labelling and documentation for customs clearance
Strong ongoing partnership – 3rd year of continuous supply
